# Code Of Conduct

Beacon is built for people in high-stress and high-risk situations. We expect the same seriousness and care in the community around it.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and patient.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Prefer clear, evidence-based discussion.
- Welcome contributors across languages, backgrounds, and skill levels.
- Avoid harassment, intimidation, or hostile behavior.

## Emergency-domain expectations

- Do not present unsafe shortcuts as verified guidance.
- Do not introduce fake AI behavior or knowingly misleading claims.
- Be extra careful with medical, survival, disaster, and security-related advice.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, open a private moderator contact through GitHub if possible. For safety or security concerns, use the path in [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md).
